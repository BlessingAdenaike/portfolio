FlexTrade

User Experience (UX) and Sales Performance Analysis for an ecommerce business

Project Overview

The project focuses on analyzing the user experience (UX) of FlexTrade, an e-commerce platform, to uncover insights and potential areas for improvement. The dashboard provided visualizes several key performance indicators (KPIs) related to user behavior and app performance. The objective is to identify the UX issues that may be hindering conversions and user engagement, such as high bounce rates, cart abandonment, and low average order values, while offering insights based on data analysis to guide actionable improvements. The data used includes app analytics, user behavior, and user feedback, and is designed to uncover trends, correlations, and insights that will help FlexTrade enhance their app to improve the user experience and ultimately drive better business outcomes.

Analysis

This Dashboard shows the correlation between the time of day and the number of page views. Peaks and troughs in traffic are visible throughout the day, which can inform strategies for optimizing user engagement during high-traffic times and addressing dips during low-traffic periods. The relationship between bounce rate and conversion rate is visualized over time, revealing how high bounce rates often coincide with low conversion rates. This suggests that users who leave the app without engaging may not be effectively converted into buyers. This analysis highlights how frequently users who add items to their cart proceed to convert (i.e., complete the purchase). A gap in this area would suggest an opportunity to reduce cart abandonment by improving the checkout experience or offering incentives to complete the purchase. The number of feedback contents, including reviews and customer surveys, indicates common user sentiments. Positive feedback may suggest areas of success, while negative comments (e.g., “app crashes,” “checkout process improvement”) can point out areas needing urgent attention. Investigating the correlation between session duration and bounce rate reveals whether shorter sessions tend to correlate with higher bounce rates, possibly indicating that users are leaving the app quickly due to frustration or poor navigation. .

Insights and Recommendations

The dashboard clearly shows periods when the bounce rate spikes. Users leaving the app quickly may be due to poor app performance, slow loading times, or irrelevant content. Addressing these issues could help improve engagement and reduce bounce rates. The gap between cart additions and completed conversions indicates a potential issue with the checkout process. Streamlining this process and ensuring that users don’t face friction during checkout could improve the conversion rate. Shorter sessions are often tied to higher bounce rates. This could be a sign that users are not finding the information or products they are looking for quickly enough. Improving the product search function and user interface could help increase session durations and reduce bounce rates. Simplify the checkout experience and ensure a smooth transition from cart to purchase. Consider adding incentives such as discounts or faster shipping options. Based on feedback and session duration analysis, improving the app's navigation and making products easier to find will likely reduce bounce rates. In conclusion, the analysis highlights key areas for improvement and provides actionable insights to enhance the UX and overall performance of the FlexTrade app, aiming to boost user engagement, reduce bounce rates, and improve conversion rates. .
